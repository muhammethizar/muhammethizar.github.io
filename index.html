Biography

Maria Sharapova is a Russian former professional tennis player and also she won five Grand Slam titles. She was one of top tennis players in the early 21st century. Basically she was a winner and she can do anything to get her goals in both personal and professional life. She is one of the most famous women athletes among the world and she was famously known as the face of women tennis. Her biggest strength was her height and her power to win the opponents. She was nicknamed as a “Screaming Cinderella” because of her grunting habit while playing in the court.
